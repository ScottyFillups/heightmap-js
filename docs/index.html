Terrapaint

A simple wrapper that lets you draw heightmaps easily.

The Terrapaint module (found on NPM) exports a wrapper that takes a noise function, along with the height and width of your canvas, and paints a heightmap!

Terrapaint comes with many options that gives you control of your heightmap, such as specifying octaves and frequencies, and setting your own custom color map.
